If you’re not familiar with CrossFit, you might think our prices are higher than your typical gym membership.

At CrossFit UOC, we programme your workouts, instruct the movements and will alter them to accommodate your current fitness level as necessary. We limit the size of our group classes which ensures that you receive the equivalent to semi-personal training.
